Faculty Advisor

Professor Qimin Yang

Engineering Department at Harvey Mudd College

Professor Yang is a Professor of Engineering at Harvey Mudd College. She recieved her BS degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Beijing University of Post and Telecommunications and her PhD from Princeton University in Electrical Engineering. She was the associate director of the Harvey Mudd College Clinic Program for several years and specialized in fiber optic communications. She has past experience with projects that aim to empower communities abroad and is a truly inspiring and motivating individual who will help grow the Harvey Mudd College Chapter of EWB.
